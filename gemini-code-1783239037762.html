CINEMATIC PRODUCTION HOUSE

UJAAN STORIES

UJAAN Stories is a cinematic production house producing documentaries, micro-documentaries, and original visual stories about people, culture, history, nature, and social issues.

Why Ujaan Stories?

We believe every place, every person, and every struggle has a story worth preserving. By going beyond the mainstream, we document voices that deserve to be seen, heard, and remembered. We are dedicated to exploring overlooked lives, forgotten histories, and the fragile beauty of nature—one story at a time.

Founder: Atiur Rahman
